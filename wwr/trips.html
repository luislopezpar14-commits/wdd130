Our Exciting Trips

Choose from our carefully curated selection of white water rafting adventures. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced rafter, we have the perfect trip for you!

Los Dientes del Diablo

Difficulty: Advanced

Duration: Full Day (8 hours)

Price: $150 per person

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush on one of the most challenging rapids in the region. Los Dientes del Diablo ("Devil's Teeth") features Class IV and V rapids that will test your skills and courage. Perfect for experienced paddlers looking for an unforgettable challenge.

Big Mallard

Difficulty: Intermediate

Duration: Half Day (4 hours)

Price: $85 per person

Big Mallard offers the perfect balance of excitement and accessibility. With Class II and III rapids, this trip is ideal for those with some rafting experience. Enjoy stunning scenery and plenty of thrilling moments along this scenic stretch of river.

Half Day Adventure

Difficulty: Beginner

Duration: Half Day (3 hours)

Price: $65 per person

Perfect for families and first-time rafters, the Half Day Adventure features calm to moderate rapids on scenic waters. Our expert guides will ensure you have a safe and memorable experience while learning essential rafting techniques.
